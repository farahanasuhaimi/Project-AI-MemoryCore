## Session 3 — 16:26 (office laptop)
*cms-takaful — focus points feature*

Back on the office laptop. Short conceptual + build session on cms-takaful. Nufa had been thinking about how reach angles and strategies should connect, and wanted to add a layer of guided talking points when executing a strategy.

**What was discussed:**
- Current structure: angles and strategies already have a `angle_strategy` pivot, `channel` and `category` already exist on strategies — the bones were mostly there.
- Missing piece: no way to capture *what to talk about* within a strategy. Nufa wanted reusable tags (not freetext) that guide the conversation focus — e.g., "Simpan untuk Masa Depan", "Ketenangan Fikiran".

**What was built:**
- `focus_points` table — `title`, `description`, `group`, `status`
- `strategy_focus_point` pivot — many-to-many between strategies and focus points
- `FocusPoint` model with `strategies()` relation
- `Strategy` model updated with `focusPoints()` relation
- `FocusPointSeeder` — 22 pre-populated focus points across 6 groups: `financial`, `protection`, `family`, `life_stage`, `emotional`, `islamic`
- Committed and pushed to GitHub (`1280c4f`)

**Note:** Migrations couldn't run on office laptop — no `.env`, SQLite driver missing. Will run `php artisan migrate` + seed on home PC.
