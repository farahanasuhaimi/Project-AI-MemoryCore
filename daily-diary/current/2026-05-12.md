### How the Strategy Marketplace Works (end-to-end)

- Seller: pins AI content → Content Library → clicks "Sell" → fills title/description/price → listed
- Buyer: browses `/marketplace/strategies` → clicks "Buy · N cr" → credits deducted, seller credited → content copied into buyer's "Marketplace Imports" reach angle
- Guard rails: can't buy own listing, can't buy twice, insufficient credits shows error flash
